Our Method

Learn vocabulary with control and your own pace.

FlashLingo combines portable decks, local-first study, recognition and production cards, write mode, audio and spaced repetition so you can build vocabulary consistently.

FocusVocabulary and example sentences
StudyRecognition and production
ProgressSpaced repetition and statistics
DataLocal-first and no account

Local learning

Your decks, progress, settings, sessions and statistics are stored on your device. You don't need an account to study.

Active practice

Cards can train comprehension and active recall. Write mode helps you check answers before grading them.

Measurable review

The algorithm schedules reviews based on performance and shows statistics to understand accuracy, upcoming load and difficult cards.

Study flow

Import, study, review and improve.

01

Import a deck

Choose a .flashjp package, review its content and prepare the cards to study locally.

02

Study cards

Practice recognition and production with definitions, sentences, translations, readings, audio and images.

03

Review on time

Spaced repetition orders new, learning, review and relearning cards within a daily queue.

04

Adjust your pace

Change limits, steps, reminders and parameters per deck to adapt the load to your goals.
